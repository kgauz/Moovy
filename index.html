Are you licensed and insured in Canada?

+

Yes — Moovy is a licensed moving company in Canada. Every move is protected by liability insurance, so your belongings are always safe.

Do you offer free quotes?

+

Absolutely. We provide free quotes online or by phone. In most cases, an accurate estimate is possible from the details you share, but in-person assessments are available if needed.

Are there hidden fees?

+

No. Our pricing is transparent — hourly rate, 3-hour minimum, 15-minute increments, plus a truck fee. There are no extra charges for stairs, long hallways, or heavy items.

What’s your cancellation or rescheduling policy?

+

We don’t require deposits, and there are no cancellation or rescheduling fees. If your plans change, just let us know as early as possible.

How far in advance should I book my move?

+

We recommend booking as soon as you know your date. Weekends and month-ends fill up quickly, so early booking gives you the best chance to secure your preferred slot.

Do you handle last-minute or emergency moves?

+

Yes — subject to availability, we can arrange urgent moves at the same reasonable rates as regular bookings.
